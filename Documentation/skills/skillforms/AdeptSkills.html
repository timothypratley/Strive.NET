                        potentials. Spirit is one of these, and the 
                        training technique to learn spirit leaves the 
                        adept with the understanding and ability to 
                        cast the spell 'dream ward'.

                        that all adepts must learn in order to be 
                        able to cast magic.

                        The spell dream ward will come free to someone

                        100% in all potential bases. Dream ward removes 
                        any dead spirits who are haunting the adept so

Linking                 Certain spells can only be cast be linked 
                        mages. These spells are usually powerful. 
                        Linking is an important part of an adepts 
                        training.

Duration                Until the mages choose to unlink themselves
Use                     Cast more powerful magic then they can cast 
                        on their own
Syntax                  Link (target player with the link skill)
                        Unlink

Affect                  This skill allows any number of players with 
                        the link skill to link to each other to cast 
                        magic. The players must be grouped.

                        Link works by combining the energy of all people
                        who are linked for the purpose of casting spells. 
                        Anyone casting a spell whilst linked will take 
                        equal amounts of energy from each of the linked 
                        players in casting the spell.

                        You receive a message when someone links with you, 
                        if you wish to remove that link just type unlink. 
                        If you or the person you are trying to link to 
                        fails in their link skill the link is not created.

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           Many powerful spells require more energy 
                                then a single mage can have.

Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   None

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 100%, costs 1 practice 
                                point per percent

Casting costs etc               None
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Only if you linked before combat started

Stone Skin              Harden skin to help prevent taking damage 
                        (skin doesn’t actually look like stone)

Duration                2 hours for every 5 levels of the caster
Use                     Provide protection against harm
Syntax                  Cast ‘stone skin’
Affect                  This skill has two affects. The first is 
                        to increase your armour class by 40 as your 
                        skin is hardened and more resistant to blows. 

                        The second is to increase your earth affinity 
                        by 20 and reduce all of your other affinities 
                        by 5 each.

Ethereal Shield         Protective barrier that will stop most 
                        projectiles (not called shots)


Duration                2 hours for every 5 levels of the caster
Use                     Provide protection against harm
Syntax                  Cast ‘ethereal shield’
Affect                  This skill has two affects. The first is to 
                        prevent all normal projectile weapons from 
                        hitting you including arrows, thrown knives 
                        and objects. This barrier will not however 
                        protect you against called shots.

                        The second is to increase your air affinity 
                        by 20 and reduce all of your other affinities 
                        by 5 each.
