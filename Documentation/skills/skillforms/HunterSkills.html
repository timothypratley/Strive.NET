Weapon technique        Basically the second attack skill, enabling 
                        those skilled in fighting to perform two attacks 
                        each round in a weapon they have trained 
                        (see above). 

Duration                Permanent.
Use                     Skilled fighters have an advantage over those 
                        not trained in the use of weaponry.

Syntax                  None
Affect                  Can perform two attacks per round. The chance of 
                        success of the second attack follows the same 
                        rules as the first attack with the exception that 
                        the boundaries for a successful hit change.

                        With a single attack you have a 25% of missing 
                        totally, a 50% of hitting their armour and 25% 
                        chance of hitting and bypassing their armour.

                        The second attack would have a 35% chance of 
                        missing, a 50% chance of hitting their armour 
                        and a 15% chance of bypassing their armour.

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Secondary Element               None

Prerequisites                   You MUST have 95% competency in the weapon 
                                type you are wielding for the skill to take 
                                affect. If you are wielding no weapon then 
                                they need the unarmed combat skill at 95%

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point 
                                for every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               Costs you an extra 5 energy every combat round 
                                that you successfully use the skill

Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Yes

Missile Weapons         Learn to effectively use crossbows and 
                        longbows etc

Duration                N/A
Use                     To allow people to train in the use of weapons 
                        and make sure that only the people who should   
                        be able to realistically use a weapon can use it.

Syntax                  Shoot (direction) (distance) (target)

                        double their existing hit roll, so twice as many 
                        tries to try and hit an opponent if the skill is

                        opponent.

                        single square in any direction.

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point

Advanced Camouflage     Only outdoor sectors, can be searched for 
                        but reduced chance (like DR search) perhaps 
                        level based

Use                     Camouflage with greater skill then normal.
Syntax                  Camouflage
Affect                  You can only use advanced camouflage in outdoor 
                        sectors.  Advanced camouflage is an advanced form 
                        of camouflaging but can be searched for. If 
                        you speak you are revealed, if you move you are 
                        revealed. If you do perform any action (unless 
                        it is stealthed somehow) you are revealed.

			The advantage of advanced camouflage over normal

Terrain Attack          (Make use of your surroundings to aid you)      

Duration                Permanent whilst you have selected that stance
Use                     To give you the edge and to provide variation 
                        in combat.
Syntax                  stance (stance type)
Affect                  If you are in terrain stance and your stance 
                        beats your opponents stance you receive a 
                        different advantage depending upon the terrain:

                        City    +4 to dexterity check to determine if 
                                attack hits or opponents attack misses.

                        Inside  Strength doubled to calculate damage.

                        Field, Road, desert     Armour cannot be 
                                                bypassed

                        Jungle, Forrest         25% chance of miss 
                                                reduced to 10%

                        Hills, Mountains        Strength tripled to 
                                                calculate damage

                        Water, Swamp    Do not suffer the penalties 
                                        that all other  people suffer 
                                        whilst fighting in water or 
                                        swampy terrain.

                        Ice     +6 to dexterity check to determine 
                                if attack hits of opponents attack misses.

Skills it counteracts           Patience stance and circle stance
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None

Prerequisites                   You MUST be using a weapon with a damage 
                                type that fits with a weapon type skill 
                                you have practiced to 95%, and you must 
                                be wielding a weapon (can't be unarmed).

                                You MUST also have combat basics trained to 95%.

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point for 
                                every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               None
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Yes
