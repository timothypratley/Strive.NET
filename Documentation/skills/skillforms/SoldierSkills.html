short blade weapons     When a person with this skill is using a short 
                        bladed weapon (ie it has the damage type of 
                        stab or pierce) they gain an advantage to 
                        their chance to hit.

Duration                N/A
Use                     To allow people to train in the use of weapons 
                        and make sure that only the people who should   
                        be able to realistically use a weapon can use it.

Syntax                  None
Affect                  Whilst wielding a short bladed weapon the person 
                        gets double their existing hit roll, so twice as 
                        many tries to try and hit an opponent if the skill 
                        is used successfully.

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   None

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point 
                                for every percent. Max % is 95.
Casting costs etc               None
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Yes

Weapon technique        Basically the second attack skill, enabling 
                        those skilled in fighting to perform two attacks 
                        each round in a weapon they have trained 
                        (see above). 

Duration                Permanent.
Use                     Skilled fighters have an advantage over those 
                        not trained in the use of weaponry.

Syntax                  None
Affect                  Can perform two attacks per round. The chance of 
                        success of the second attack follows the same 
                        rules as the first attack with the exception that 
                        the boundaries for a successful hit change.

                        With a single attack you have a 25% of missing 
                        totally, a 50% of hitting their armour and 25% 
                        chance of hitting and bypassing their armour.

                        The second attack would have a 35% chance of 
                        missing, a 50% chance of hitting their armour 
                        and a 15% chance of bypassing their armour.

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Secondary Element               None

Prerequisites                   You MUST have 95% competency in the weapon 
                                type you are wielding for the skill to take 
                                affect. If you are wielding no weapon then 
                                they need the unarmed combat skill at 95%

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point 
                                for every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               Costs you an extra 5 energy every combat round 
                                that you successfully use the skill

Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Yes
